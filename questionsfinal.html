Q: What should I do if my dog is choking?

A: Check if you can safely remove the object with your fingers. If not, perform the Heimlich maneuver by applying pressure to the abdomen just behind the ribcage. Call a vet immediately.


Q: What should I do if my cat is injured?

A: Keep the cat calm and still, wrap it gently in a towel, and transport it to the nearest veterinarian. Avoid touching open wounds.


Q: What are the initial steps to care for a rescue pet?

A: Schedule a vet visit, set up a regular feeding routine, and give the pet time to adjust to their new environment.


Q: What vaccinations does my pet need?

A: Dogs typically need vaccines for rabies, distemper, and parvovirus, while cats require vaccines for rabies, feline distemper, and feline leukemia. Consult your vet for a tailored schedule.


Q: How often should I clean my pet's food and water bowls?

A: Wash food bowls after every meal and water bowls daily to prevent bacteria buildup.
