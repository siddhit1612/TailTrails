Volunteer

Tagging Stray Dogs for Identification

Hosted by: PAWS Mumbai. Location: Powai

Activity: Volunteers are tasked with tagging and reflecting collars on stray dogs in the Powai area to improve their visibility and ensure easy identification for future vaccination and feeding drives.

First-Aid and Rescue Assistance

Hosted by: Animals Matter to Me (AMTM). Location: Andheri East

Activity: Volunteers are trained to administer basic first aid to injured stray animals and assist in transporting animals to shelters or clinics for advanced treatment.

Stray Dog Vaccination Camp

Hosted by: In Defence of Animals (IDA), Mumbai. Location: Malad

Activity: Volunteers assist in organizing stray dog vaccination camps in Malad by bringing dogs to the camp, calming them during vaccinations, and recording their details for follow-ups.

Adoption Drive Assistant

Hosted by: World For All Animal Care and Adoptions. Location: Juhu

Activity: Volunteers help organize adoption drives, preparing animals, speaking to potential adopters, and ensuring the adoption process runs smoothly.

Feeding Stray Animals

Hosted by: PAWS Mumbai. Location: Mulund East

Activity: Join a team to distribute food and water to stray dogs and cats in the area. Volunteers also educate locals about feeding strays responsibly.

Event: Shelter Enrichment Day

Hosted by: BSPCA Mumbai. Location: Parel

Activity: Spend a day at the shelter enriching the lives of rescued animals by playing with them, helping clean their enclosures, and donating or creating toys for mental stimulation.

Stray Animal Tagging and Tracking

Hosted by: World For All Animal Care and Adoptions. Location: Powai

Activity: Volunteers attach reflective collars to stray dogs to improve their visibility at night and help track their movements for follow-up care.

Community Awareness Workshop

Hosted by: Save Our Strays (SOS).Location: Dadar West

Activity: Help organize workshops to educate the community on caring for stray animals, promoting responsible feeding, and understanding animal behavior.
